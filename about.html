About Owais Kitchen Restaurant

Our journey in creating unforgettable dining experiences!

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Owais Kitchen began with a simple vision: to serve fresh, delicious, and high-quality meals in a warm, inviting atmosphere. What started as a small neighborhood bistro has grown into a beloved culinary destination, thanks to our commitment to exceptional ingredients and heartfelt hospitality.

Our chefs carefully craft each dish with love and expertise, blending traditional techniques with innovative flavors to create memorable dining experiences for every guest.

Kazoori Fabriz

Head Chef

Meet Our Head Chef

With over 20 years of mastery in Italian and French cuisine, Chef Kazoori brings unparalleled expertise to our kitchen. Trained in Michelin-starred restaurants across Europe, he combines technical precision with creative flair.

His philosophy: "Great food begins with respect - for ingredients, for tradition, and most importantly, for the people we serve."

  • James Beard Award Nominee (2018, 2020)
  • Author of Mediterranean Flavors Reimagined
  • Trained in Paris, Rome, and Barcelona
