Margherita Pizza

$10.99

Classic Italian pizza features a thin, crispy crust topped with a rich tomato sauce, creamy mozzarella cheese, and fresh cherry tomatoes.

The pizza is garnished with fresh basil leaves, adding a fragrant and slightly peppery flavor. The combination of red tomatoes, white mozzarella, and green basil represents the colors of the Italian flag, making it a simple yet flavorful dish. Perfect for lovers of authentic and fresh ingredients!
